Noble Team (Halo Reach)

Noble Team, was a special fireteam sized unit of the UNSC Special Warfare Command Group Three, composed mostly of SPARTAN-III supersoldiers and one SPARTAN-II commando as of 2552.

Composition and status members:

  • Commander Carter-A259 (Noble One), a no-nonsense soldier, Carter served as the leader of Noble Team. Listed MIA. Confirmed KIA in an intentional Pelican crash during the Fall of Reach.
  • Lieutenant Commander Catherine-B320 (Noble Two), curious and intrepid second-in-command, Kat served as the intelligence specialist. Listed MIA. Confirmed KIA by a Needle Rifle during the Fall of Reach.
  • Warrant Officer Jun-A266 (Noble Three), a thoughtful and taciturn soldier, Jun served as the sniper of Noble Team. Jun is confirmed to be alive and helped handpick the very first SPARTAN-IV.
  • Warrant Officer Emile-A239 (Noble Four), a bold and aggressive Spartan, Emile was the assault specialist of the team. Listed MIA. Confirmed KIA by Energy Sword of Sangheili Zealots during the Fall of Reach.
  • Chief Warrant Officer Jorge-052 (Noble Five), the only Spartan-II on the team, Jorge and his "humane" personality served as the team's heavy weapons specialist. Listed MIA. Confirmed KIA during Operation: UPPER CUT.
  • Lieutenant SPARTAN-B312 (Noble Six), a new addition to the team, this Spartan's background is mostly unknown. They have no specified role, known only as the team's 'Hyper-Lethal Vector. Listed MIA. Confirmed KIA by Sangheili warriors sometime towards the end of the Fall of Reach.
